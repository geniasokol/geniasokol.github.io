Welcome


Need something translated from Russian to English?

I can help! I have been translating professionally for about five years and am currently working on my M.A. in Translation at Kent State University. With my native command of both Russian and English, combined with a strong background in translation theory and practice, I have a unique advantage in providing high-quality translations with fast turnaround times. I also provide several related services, such as editing, transcription, and formatting.

I am an associate member of the American Translators Association and am listed in their directory. I am active in the Slavic Language Division.

I work with both agencies and direct clients. If you think you might have a project for me, please feel free to contact me - contact info is listed below and on the Contact Me page. I look forward to working with you!
