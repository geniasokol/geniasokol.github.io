What services do I provide?

Translation

Have a text in one language and need it to be in another? That's what translation is for! I translate in the following language pairs:

  • Russian to English
  • French to English
  • English to Russian

I accept files in Word, OpenOffice, and PDF format, and am able to match formatting (except graphics) if necessary.

Editing (English only)

If you have a text in English that doesn't read right, I can make it sound like English again! My specialty is in academic writing, but I can edit any kind of text.

Transcription

Sometimes you may want the transcript or translation of a video file: an interview, a TV broadcast, or a film. Or you may want audio: a radio program, a podcast, or even music. Transcription will give you the text equivalent of what is said, in the same language.
