Welcome


My primary service is Russian to English translation, but I also offer a variety of other services.

Translation

My preferred language direction is Russian to English. However, I also have experience translating English to Russian, as well as French to English. Please see the Qualifications page for my background in each of these languages.

CAT Tools: I use SDL Trados Studio 2015 and SDL Passolo. I do not currently offer discounts for fuzzy matches of any level except 100% and Context Match.

My specializations include:

  • Legal translation, particularly contracts and laws
  • Journalism and essays
  • Academic writing, particularly linguistics, history, and other social sciences

Editing and Proofreading

I can edit English texts, including: translations by another translator, texts written by non-native English speakers, or texts that need to conform to a particular style guide.

Machine Translation Post-Editing

I am also happy to work with machine translation output, and can edit lightly or heavily according to your specifications.

Formatting

I have extensive experience formatting in Microsoft Word, as well as some exposure to InDesign. I can recreate the formatting of documents in .pdf format, including complex tables and layouts.

Rates and Fees

Rate and payment information is available upon request. Please be advised that I typically charge a $25 minimum for all projects and may apply rush fees for same-day delivery and other special cases.
